Generic Medicines

Generic medicines can be classiied into several categories based on their therapeutic use or mechanism of action. Some common categories of generic medicines include: Thyroid, Diabetes, Neurology, Gynaecology, Analgesics and pain relievers, Anti-inflammatory drugs,Antibiotics and antivirals, Antihypertensives and cardiovascula drugs and others. These categories are not exhaustive, and there are many other types of generic medicines available for various medical conditions. It's important to consult with a healthcare professional to determine the most appropriate medication for a specific health concern.
